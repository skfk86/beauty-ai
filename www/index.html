Privacy Policy
Rosiva — Gilgamesh LLC
🔒
Your Privacy is Our Priority
Welcome to Rosiva. At Gilgamesh LLC, we are committed to providing a smart and secure experience that fully protects your privacy and personal data.
1. Voice Data
Rosiva relies on a smart voice assistant dedicated to beauty and personal skincare needs. We request microphone access to process your instant queries about makeup, fragrances and skincare. This voice is analyzed in real time by AI to provide answers and recommendations. We do not save or permanently store voice recordings.
2. Personalized Recommendations
The app provides personalized recommendations and explanations of product ingredients and their benefits based on your text or voice preferences. We follow the principle of "No photos — No analysis" completely; we never request camera access and never capture or analyze your facial features.
3. Purchase Links & Third Parties
The app includes official and direct purchase links from trusted global retailers such as Sephora, Ulta, Amazon, Dior, Notino and Lookfantastic. When you click these links you are redirected to external platforms governed by privacy policies entirely independent of our app.
4. Security Guarantees — Our Commitments
We never ask for your photos
Your privacy is 100% protected. No need to upload personal photos.
We never sell your data
Your data is completely safe and is not shared or sold to any advertising entity or third parties.
Full control over your history
You can delete your account and clear all conversations and voice queries at any time.
5. Disclaimer
All recommendations, analyses and information provided through the Rosiva AI assistant are for general beauty and skincare guidance only. This information does not constitute medical advice or a therapeutic diagnosis for skin problems, and the company bears no responsibility for the results of product use. Always consult specialists or official product packaging.
